## MUST-NOT-FORGET

- Summary section mandatory - copy/paste ready list at document top
- Label ALL findings: `[ASSUMED]` `[VERIFIED]` `[TESTED]` `[PROVEN]`
- MEPI by default (2-3 curated options), MCPI when completeness needed
- Deep beats wide - 2-3 well-researched options over 10+ superficial
- Document exclusions - what was NOT considered and why
- Keep ALL sources - even if findings were minimal

## Workflow

1. Make research plan, add sections step by step to INFO document
2. After each section, ask: Need further verification? Duplicates? Unverified/contradicting findings? Actually helpful?
3. Review new sections in context of existing ones - remove cognitive overload, redundancies, ambiguities
4. If no verified solution exists, think outside the box - different perspectives, clever alternatives
5. Never pollute document with non-working solutions or irrelevant research
6. List all sources with URL and primary findings at document end
7. Summarize findings as copy/paste ready list at document top

**Source Retention Rule:** ALL sources must be kept. Never discard - document what was checked to avoid re-checking.
