TechCorp Inc.'s Annual Report for 2024 highlights a successful year marked by significant growth and strategic initiatives. The company achieved a revenue of $45.2 million, reflecting a 23% increase from 2023, with a net profit of $8.7 million and a workforce of 342 employees. Notably, TechCorp launched a new AI product line that alone generated $12 million in revenue, and the company expanded its presence into three international markets: Germany, Japan, and Brazil.

In the CEO's statement, the transformative impact of strategic investments in AI technology was emphasized, suggesting that these efforts have positioned TechCorp for ongoing growth into 2025. The report also noted an impressive improvement in customer satisfaction, reaching a score of 94%. Key members of the Board of Directors include Chair Sarah Chen, Michael Rodriguez, Emily Watson, and David Kim, who have overseen these advancements.
