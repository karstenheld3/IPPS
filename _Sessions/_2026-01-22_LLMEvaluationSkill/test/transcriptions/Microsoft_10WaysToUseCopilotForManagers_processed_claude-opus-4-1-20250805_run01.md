# 10 ways managers can use Microsoft 365 Copilot

Leave the busy work to Copilot and focus on being the best manager you can be

## 1. Lead better meetings
Catch up and prepare for meetings with your team members using Business Chat.

✨ **Prompt:** Help me prepare for [meeting].

## 2. Keep everyone on track
List action items and owners from team meetings using Microsoft 365 Copilot in Teams.

✨ **Prompt:** List action items.

## 3. Communicate effectively
Write an announcement to your team using Business Chat.

✨ **Prompt:** Write an email to my team about our top priorities for next quarter from [file].

## 4. Coach team members
Learn new coaching and management skills using Business Chat.

✨ **Prompt:** Give me some tips for helping my team self-advocate.

## 5. Foster team innovation
Generate and organize new ideas using Microsoft 365 Copilot in a team Loop page.

✨ **Prompt:** Brainstorm strategies my team can use to increase user engagement with our product.

## 6. Compile research for your team
Compile research and insights from across the web for your team using Business Chat.

*Tip: Use the "Web" toggle in Business Chat to source responses from web data.*

✨ **Prompt:** Summarize trends and news on [topic] from the last few months.

## 7. Catch up on your inbox
Summarize your inbox or a long email thread using Microsoft 365 Copilot in Outlook.

✨ Select "**Summarize**" in an open email thread.

## 8. Onboard team members
Draft welcome and onboarding documents using Microsoft 365 in Word.

✨ **Prompt:** Create an onboarding document that lists the steps a new employee can take in their first month of their role as a [new-in-career designer].

## 9. Create executive reports
Generate drafts for executive reports and updates using Business Chat.

✨ **Prompt:** Write an executive report summarizing the goals, target audience, progress, and expected impact of [project] based on [file].

## 10. Brainstorm team activities
Generate ideas for team building activities using Business Chat.

*Tip: Use the "Web" toggle in Business Chat to source responses from web data.*

✨ **Prompt:** List ideas for a fun remote team building event.

---

Explore more prompts in Copilot Lab by selecting "**View prompts**" next to the Copilot prompt box in your favorite Microsoft 365 apps.
