# SSE Business Model

## Page Content

**How we create value: Our Business Model**

We are developing, building, operating and investing in our unique portfolio of assets across the electricity value chain.

### How We Are Structured

**Sections (Left to Right):**

1. Renewables
   - SSE Renewables: Providing clean and affordable home-grown energy

2. Flexibility
   - SSE Thermal: Balancing the market with flexible generation

3. Networks
   - SSEN Transmission: Enabling net zero by connecting renewables to centres of demand

4. Distribution
   - SSEN Distribution: Bringing net zero to the doorstep by decarbonising streets and towns

### Energy Products and Services

1. Energy Customer Solutions
   - SSE Airtricity and SSE Business Energy provide a shopfront for the Group's renewable generation output

2. SSE Enterprise
   - Bringing innovative solutions to business markets

3. SSE Energy Markets
   - Trading commodities, securing value for SSE assets and managing volume

4. Corporate Services
   - Providing cost-effective shared services and strategic direction across the Group

### What We Do

Drive progress towards net zero by developing, building, operating and investing in clean, secure and affordable electricity infrastructure needed for the energy system of tomorrow.

**Circular Process:**
- Develop
- Build
- Operate
- Invest
