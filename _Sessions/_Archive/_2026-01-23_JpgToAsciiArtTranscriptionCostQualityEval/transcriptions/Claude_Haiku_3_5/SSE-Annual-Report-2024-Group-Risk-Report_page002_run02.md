### Sections

#### How We Are Structured
1. Renewables (SSE Renewables)
   - Providing clean and affordable home-grown energy
2. SSE Thermal
   - Balancing the market with flexible generation
3. SSEN Transmission
   - Enabling net zero by connecting renewables to centres of demand
4. SSEN Distribution
   - Bringing net zero to the doorstep by decarbonising streets and towns

#### Energy Products and Services
1. Energy Customer Solutions
   - Providing flexible energy solutions for renewable generation output
2. SSE Enterprise
   - Bringing innovative low-carbon solutions to business markets
3. SSE Energy Markets
   - Trading commodities, securing value for SSE assets and managing volatility
4. Corporate Services
   - Providing cost-effective shared services and strategic direction across the Group

### What We Do
- Drive progress towards net zero by developing, building, operating and investing in clean, secure and affordable electricity infrastructure needed for the energy system of tomorrow.

### Business Cycle Diagram
- Cycle Components:
  1. Develop
  2. Invest
  3. Build
  4. Operate

### The Value We Create
1. €14,000* Direct Employees
2. €12.2m Investment in Communities
3. €2.5bn Investment in Net Zero Infrastructure
4. €5m Customers Served
5. €5.5bn Supplier Spend
6. 60p Dividend (full year)
