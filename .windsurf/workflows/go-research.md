## Rules

- **Source Retention Rule:** ALL sources (web URLs, PDFs, documents, code files) must be kept in the research output. Never discard sources - even if findings were minimal or contradictory, document what was checked to avoid re-checking.
- Think outside of the box if no verified solution exists: Are missing important points to see the problem or task from a different perspective? Are there clever alternatives that we are overlooking? 
- Don't pollute with irrelevant or non-working solutions, just don't be a super smart moron.
